Welcome to Electric Circuit Lab!


This lab is designed to help students explore and understand the principles of electric circuits, including both DC and AC systems. Built as an open-source platform, it eliminates the need for external software installations, making learning more accessible and convenient. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner, this space offers a creative and hands-on environment to experiment, analyze, and strengthen your knowledge of electrical circuits. Get ready to discover, innovate, and develop a deeper appreciation for the world of electrical engineering.
